# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Please be respectful and constructive in all project interactions.

Examples of positive behavior:
- Giving helpful, specific feedback
- Respecting different viewpoints
- Assuming good intent

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, hate speech, or personal attacks.

## Enforcement
Maintainers may remove or moderate comments, issues, or contributions that violate this policy.

## Scope
This applies to project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions, and commit comments).
